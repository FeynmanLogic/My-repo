Projects

Here are some of my notable works in Computer Architecture and AI

Transarch

A unified architectural optimization for transformers, integrating prefetching, Processing in Memory, and software-side optimizations. Developed under the guidance of Dr. Sai Manoj of George Mason University. The prefetcher has been implemented with Echo state networks.

KANBoost

A novel prefetcher approach using Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks. Developed as part of my final-year project, led by a team of four under the supervision of Dr. Sai Manoj and Prof. R.P. Gohil. Arxiv here

View on GitHub

Translearn

Optimization of transformers using Active Learning and Knowledge Distillation techniques, developed under the guidance of Dr. Chandra Prakash at SVNIT, Surat. (Very recently accepted at the ICCI 2024!!) Presentation certificate here Slides here Paper pdf here

View on GitHub

Echofast
